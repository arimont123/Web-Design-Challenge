Wind Speed vs. Latitude

Summary: This plot shows how the wind speed changes throughout different parts of the world. From the graph, there is not much of a correlation between wind speed and latitude. However, we can see that the majority of cities have a wind speed between 0 and 10 mph. There are few cities that have wind speeds above 25 mph and these are all located in the Northern Hempisphere with higher latitudes.
